WHAT'S AROUND

Switzerland

As the world leader in travel experiences since 1947, you'll connect to the real Switzerland in a way like no other. Consume smooth milk chocolate in the foothills of the Swiss Alps. Ride a horse and carriage through alpine forests, and spend hours adrift on idyllic lakes.

Brazil

Its sun-drenched landscapes range from hectic cities to hip surfer towns, powdery white-sand beaches and wild rainforest. Rio de Janeiro is understandably the focus of most traveller itineraries. Wander the lively streets of Santa Teresa, ascend Sugarloaf Mountain to stand at the feet of Christ the Redeemer, and take in the stunning panoramic views.

Vietnam

Misty mountains, cascading rice terraces, thick jungle, unspoiled beaches and two of Southeast Asia's most time-honoured cities -it's hard to know where to begin. For most visitors, it all starts - or ends - in Hanoi, the chaotic capital of the north. Here, you'll find holy monasteries beside bustling markets, manic streets and towering skyscrapers.
